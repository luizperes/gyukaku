Twelve Principles of Gyu-Kaku Philosophy


1. Be honest

2. Have passion in all we do

3. Work harder than anyone else

4. Always aim for the best result

5. Be humble

6. Never forget to be appreciative

7. Be able to admit fault

8. Be the strictest to myself

9. Have pride in my work

10. I am the company

11. Never give up

12. Have high civility
